Quick-Scroll

Scroll to the top of the page on single click and to the bottom on double click. Quick Scroll is a Chrome extension designed to enhance your browsing experience by allowing you to quickly navigate to the top or bottom of any webpage. With a simple click, you can instantly scroll to the top, and with a double click, you can jump to the bottom. It's perfect for quickly accessing different parts of long articles, social media feeds, or extensive documents.
