Kimiko

Kimiko (キミコ) is a high-end wallet analysis engine and compliance toolkit for Solana. It empowers users with Selective Visibility, allowing them to measure their "Exposure Surface" and prove their legitimacy without revealing their entire financial history. By leveraging Inco Shield (FHE), Kimiko generates trustless, encrypted identity attestations that bridge the gap between user privacy and institutional compliance requirements.

Key Highlights:

  • Privacy Scoring: Quantifies on-chain traceability using a bespoke heuristic classifier that detects "Leakage Vectors" like CEX timing correlations.
  • Inco Shield (FHE): Utilizes Fully Homomorphic Encryption via the Inco Lightning network to produce encrypted on-chain reputations.
  • Behavioral Compliance: Replaces traditional KYC with "Compliance Tiers", verifying users based on historical behavior.
  • The Zen Interface: A custom-designed tactical "Digital Zen" HUD that translates raw hex data into human-readable risk assessments.
