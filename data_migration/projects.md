## OnlyPngs
- **Category:** Web3 / AI
- **Date:** 4 February, 2026
- **Hackathon:** Winner of 2:47PM Studio Hackathon
- **Tech Stack:** Avalanche, Gemini 2.0 Flash, 0xgasless AgentKit, ERC-4337 (Account Abstraction)
- **Description:** An AI-powered, non-custodial NFT trading platform enabling automated, gasless transactions.
- **Key Features:**
    - **The Sleeping Buy:** Autonomous monitoring system that executes trades based on price alerts while offline.
    - **Gasless UI/UX:** Completely gas-free experience via 0xgasless Paymaster-funded transactions.
    - **Natural Language Interface:** Uses Gemini 2.0 to translate human intent (e.g., "Buy if price drops") into on-chain actions.
    - **Security First:** Architected using Smart Accounts and session keys for non-custodial control.
